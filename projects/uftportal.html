Uft Research Portal

The UFT Research Portal is a CRUD app built in response to a mock client project exercise at Dev Bootcamp. The client was in need of a web app where university faculty and students could log in and manage their respective research proposals and experiments. The app was a group project built over the course of 3 days in Rails, backed by a PostgreSQL database, and I served as team lead for the project.

Key functionality:

  • User must log in or register to view any part of the site
  • User must register with a uft.edu email address
  • Ability to search proposals by department
  • Stars show next to proposals owned by the logged in user
  • Only faculty have the option to submit a new proposal
  • Only researchers have the option to begin a new experiment on a proposal
  • Only the owner of a proposal or experiment can edit/delete/comment on it
  • Researchers can view all proposals, but only the experiments that they own
  • Experiment page includes progress bar that is automatically updated based on the current stage of the experiment
  • A user's profile contains toggling links to all of their owned proposals/experiments
