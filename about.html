hey, i'm dana


I've spent my whole career in the tech industry, but unfortunately, in the wrong job. After 6 years of doing not-my-dream-job, I realized I couldn't possibly spend the next 40 years the same way. Problem was, I didn't know what I'd rather do instead. So I took a chance; I quit and spent a year traveling, hoping to figure out what my dream job was along the way.

Through the inevitable self-reflection that comes with 24-hour solo bus journeys, I decided to act on the hunch that I might enjoy web development. I committed five months of my life to Dev Bootcamp, promising to put nothing ahead of my number one priority: to learn as much as I possibly could.

On a personal note, I'm a cat lady who's passionate about avocados and adventure. I'm constantly thinking about how things work or became the way they are. I'm a music person, not a movie person, with the exception of documentaries. I'll always go out of my way for a great view. I eat Spaghettios a lot.
