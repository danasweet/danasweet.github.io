hi! I'm Dana.

I'm an aspiring web developer who discovered a love for programming after leaving behind an unfulfilling career in favor of traveling indefinitely. I wandered until I found what I was looking for: a job that I could wake up every morning excited about. Something challenging and frustrating and rewarding and empowering.

With a rejuvenated outlook on my future, I signed on at Dev Bootcamp and spent 5 months cramming as much code as I could into my brain. Today, I know the basics, but I'm hungry for more. I'm an ardent believer in the power of technology, and I'm thrilled to have the skills to harness it to better the world.

Other than code, my passions include cats, avocados, and adventure. I believe a documentary a day keeps boredom away. I'll always go out of my way for a great view, and I do my best thinking in a hammock. I listen to 90s music and eat Spaghettios a lot. I can do anything after two cups of coffee.
